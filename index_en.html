Server-Rules

In addition to the following rules, we expect a respectful and friendly dealing with each other

During the event we do not llow any raid!tipp for tribes: place a Pulmonoscorpius (aggressive) in your base to get a tribe log entry.

Fairplay
Exploitation of game-bugs or any other type to get an unfair advantage are prohibited. Please communicate friendly in the chat. Sexual innuendo or racist content will have a ban as a consequence. We are open to german and english text in the chat. Please take consideration for new players. We would like to keep them, not to drive out them!
advertising and Chat
Each try of advertisement or woo away of our brave survivors to other game server has consequences. Writing when using the capslock is prohibited. We reserve, to invite you to modify your name.

It is everyday life doing one raid per tribe per day. But there should be at least days between this and another raid of the same tribe. Individual solutions for those of you who cannot wait to hit each other are possible.

 

Raids
Extensive raids oder harassments by attacks, to take away the fun for others are not allowed. Everybody should enjoy this game!
  • Killing passive dinos is allowed, but is frowned upon.
  • Griefing, ie. permanent raids or botherings are not allowed, too.
rules when building
  • ShigoIsland has only one cave. To build inside the cave is not permitted.
  • Each tribe is only allowed to build 4 dino plattforms.
  • Unused structures (eg. foundations) should be destroyed. This increases the server performance. Hence, this increases your gaming experience here too. Please do not build more than 3 main bases.

When there infractions of the rules just contact the admins (send a screenshot). We will talk to each party .
